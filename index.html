How it works

Using Repitto is incredibly simple and efficient. With just a swipe of a card, you can easily navigate through the app's intuitive interface. When you come across a question, swipe left if you know the answer. Repitto will recognize your familiarity and remove that question from future training rounds, allowing you to focus on areas that require more attention.

If a question is new to you, simply swipe right, and Repitto will reveal the correct answer, helping you expand your knowledge. This interactive approach ensures that you engage with each question effectively, reinforcing your understanding of key concepts.

Quick test

To kickstart your learning journey, begin with a quick test consisting of 20 thoughtfully selected questions. This initial assessment will provide an understanding of your current knowledge level and highlight areas that may require further study.

Start your training

Once you've completed the quick test, Repitto offers a comprehensive training module with its extensive database of 600+ questions. As you swipe through the questions, the training process becomes automated, incorporating a seamless spaced repetition technique. With each round, you'll notice a decrease in the number of questions presented. This is because questions you've swiped right on, indicating mastery, are considered learned and are removed from subsequent training sets.

Exam simulator

Get the authentic exam experience with Repitto's Exam Simulator, designed to mimic the real AWS Cloud Practitioner exam CLF-C01. With a comprehensive set of 65 exam-level questions, the simulator puts your knowledge and skills to the test under realistic conditions. As you complete the exam, Repitto provides you with a detailed performance report, highlighting your strengths and areas that may require further improvement. This valuable feedback allows you to focus your future study efforts and fine-tune your exam preparation strategy.
